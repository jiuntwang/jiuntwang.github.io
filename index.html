About

Originally from Southern California, I've moved across the country a few times to experience different communities, tech, and food in each city. For my B.A., I studied Graphic Design at the University of Buffalo. Afterwards, I worked for a few years as a designer in the apparel and jewelry industry.

Recently this past year, I decided to go back to school for a Certificate in Web Development at Seattle University to expand my knowledge about current coding in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, popular task runners, plug-ins, and frameworks used in the development community.

The creation of a WordPress website


To create Cooking Medley, I used Digital Ocean as a cloud-based hosting service and installed WordPress with LAMP on Ubuntu 16.04 server. First, I created a MySQL database and user for WordPress and then installed additional PHP extensions to communicate with MYSQL. Next, I adjusted Apache's configuration to allow .htaccess overrides and rewrites. Afterwards I download WordPress using the shell/terminal and configured the WordPress directory to adjust ownership and permissions. Lastly, after a the server was set-up, a theme was added and configured to fit the needs and purpose of an online recipe book.

Client:
Cookling Medley (Personal)
