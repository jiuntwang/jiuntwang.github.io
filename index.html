About

Originally from Southern California, I've moved across the country a few times to experience a different lifestyle. For my B.A., I studied Graphic Design at the University of Buffalo. Afterwards, I worked for a few years as a designer in the apparel and jewelry industry.

Recently within the past year, I decided to go back to school for a Certificate in Web Development at Seattle University to expand my knowledge about coding in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, popular task runners, plug-ins, and frameworks used in the development community today.
